ABOUT US

MISSION

We are committed to crafting locally sourced, all-natural beverages that not only delight the palate but nourish the body and soul, fostering a spirit of community resilience and environmental sustainability.

HISTORY

Established in 2010, Bayani Brew emerges as a trailblazing beacon of social entrepreneurship, originating from the heart of the Gawad Kalinga Enchanted Farm community. Rooted in a profound commitment to empowering Filipino farmers and championing sustainable agriculture, Bayani Brew stands as a testament to the transformative potential of grassroots initiatives.
